### Storova — Your Store. Everywhere.

Millions of Indian sellers run real businesses entirely through Instagram DMs. No store. No system. Just chaos.

**Storova gives them:**

🔗 &nbsp;Own store link — `storova.shop/name`  
💸 &nbsp;UPI + card payments  
🔔 &nbsp;Instant WhatsApp alerts  
📦 &nbsp;Order tracking  
📊 &nbsp;Daily sales summary  
📱 &nbsp;100% from phone  

> *Zero tech knowledge needed.*
